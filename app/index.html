Welcome to cryptoterrorist.net

To show non-technical people who can see what in an email conversation, we have prepared 4 views for you to show.

  • The sender can write an email and send it. SSL and PGP/GPG are optional.
  • The receiver gets the mail and shows it. Decryption is optional.
  • The server obviously sees the mail contents even if SSL has been used.
  • The attacker can not see or decrypt the mail content if sent via SSL and/or GPG.

Please open the following views:

  • Sender (Mail Client)
  • Receiver of the mail
  • One mailserver
  • Attacker View
